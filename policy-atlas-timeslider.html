About this proof of concept
An illustrative animation of recreational-cannabis legalization by state, 2012–2026. Passage years are approximate and meant to tell the story of the wave — this is one layer of the planned policy atlas. Future layers: medical cannabis, psychedelic reform (Denver 2019, Oregon 2020, Colorado 2022…), penalties, research, and harm-reduction laws — plus filters and a natural-language "ask the map" feature. Educational only, not legal advice.
