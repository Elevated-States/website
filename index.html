About

Curiosity,
made useful.

The Elevated States Project is psychedelic edutainment with a purpose: make the science engaging, then bring the right people together to turn it into real change.

Why we exist

The middle ground

There's never been more interest in altered states — or more noise around them. The conversation swings between hype and fear, and the real science gets lost in the middle. We make that science engaging and accessible, and connect the people who can turn understanding into safer experiences and smarter policy.

How we work

Two engines
A

Edutainment

We turn dense science into events, talks, and content people actually enjoy — because curiosity is the gateway to literacy.

B

Convening

We put researchers, clinicians, funders, artists, and advocates in one room, then help good ideas become research, programs, and policy.

What we believe

At the intersection of curiosity, evidence, freedom & growth.
